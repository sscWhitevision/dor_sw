Careers

SW is one of the leading networks of accounting and consulting firms in the world, with a reputation for excellence and fast growth in the Asia-Pacific region. With our expanding scale and clients, we provide you with the quality and variety of work and the opportunities to excel. We foster a culture rich in collaboration and continuous learning, which empowers each individual to create genuine relationships with different stakeholders, including high quality clients and colleagues. Attracting and retaining the best people is our core strategy. We recognize that every employee is integral to our success. At SW, you will find exceptional career opportunities offered to aspiring graduates and experienced hires. We will proactively guide you towards a rewarding experience at every level and in every office around the world. We are looking for talented, driven and innovative people who can help us achieve our vision of establishing a trusted global brand. In return, we offer a powerful platform that cultivates your professional and personal goals. We provide you with competitive benefits and compensation package, and foster a supportive, motivating and inclusive environment. Our continued success relies on our ability to offer training and development for every role and every level.
